FAQ

常见问题

你们有固定店面吗？ | Do you have a fixed studio?
目前没有固定店面。所有体验皆为预约制，我们会根据人数、地区与体验形式协调适合地点。
Not at the moment. All sessions are by appointment, and locations are arranged based on group size, area, and session type.
需要有冥想或颂钵经验吗？
不需要。体验会以简单、放松、容易进入的方式进行。你只需要坐着或躺下，跟随现场节奏聆听即可。
No experience is needed. The session is beginner-friendly; simply sit or lie down comfortably and listen.
体验时要穿什么？ | What should I wear?
建议穿宽松、舒适的衣服。可以准备袜子、外套或小毯子，让身体保持舒服。
Wear loose and comfortable clothing. Socks, a light jacket, or a small blanket can help you stay comfortable.
你们可以到办公室或活动场地吗？
可以。我们可为企业活动、workshop、retreat、小型聚会或品牌活动安排体验。实际安排会视地点、人数与空间条件而定。
Yes. Sessions can be arranged for offices, workshops, retreats, small gatherings, and brand events, subject to location, group size, and space suitability.
孕妇或有特殊身体状况可以参加吗？
请在预约前告知我们，并先咨询医生或合格医疗专业人士，再决定是否适合参加。
Please inform us before booking and consult your doctor or qualified healthcare professional first.
颂钵体验是治疗吗？ | Is this a treatment?
不是。我们的体验仅用于 wellness、relaxation 和自我照顾，不提供诊断、治疗或医疗建议。
No. Our sessions are for wellness, relaxation, and self-care only. We do not provide diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice.
可以拍照发 Instagram 吗？
可以。体验前后欢迎拍照。体验进行中请保持安静，并尊重其他参与者的隐私。
Yes, before or after the session. During the session, we keep the space quiet and respect everyone’s privacy.

免责声明 | Disclaimer

本网站内容及所有颂钵体验仅供 wellness、relaxation 与自我照顾用途，不构成医疗建议、诊断或治疗。颂钵体验不能替代医生、心理健康专业人士或任何合格医疗人员的建议。如你有任何身体或心理健康相关疑问，请先咨询专业人士。

All website content and singing bowl sessions are intended for wellness, relaxation, and self-care purposes only. They do not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and are not a substitute for care from a doctor, mental health professional, or qualified healthcare provider.
